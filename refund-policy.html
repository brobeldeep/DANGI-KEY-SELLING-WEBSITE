Returns, Refund & Cancellation Policy

We aim to provide you with a seamless shopping experience. Please read the details of our returns, refunds, and cancellation policy below.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed only if the product key you purchased is found to be defective or invalid. To request a refund, you must contact us within 30 days of your purchase with valid proof of defect.

Returns

Since our product keys are digital and delivered electronically, we do not accept returns. Ensure that you check all the details before making a purchase.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are allowed if made before the product key is delivered. Once the product key has been sent to your registered email, cancellations are no longer possible.
